What's HoxTok?

HoxTok or "Hawks Talk" focuses on data visualization for all things Seahawks, as well as the rest of the NFL. We also offer commentary on the state of the NFL as well in the form of power rankings, which although seemingly biased at first, are merely accurate representations of how dominant the Seahawks have been over the last 4 years. We hope you enjoy our findings and how they are represented, despite your preconceived ESPN-fed notions of east coast superiority. Go Hawks.
