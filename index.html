What's HoxTok?

HoxTok or "Hawks Talk" focuses on data visualization for all things Seahawks, as well as the rest of the NFL. I also offer commentary on the state of the NFL as well in the form of power rankings, which although seemingly biased at first, is merely an objetive and accurate representation of how dominant the Hawks truly are. I hope viewing this data in the forms presented proves useful to you, and if you have any suggestions for data you'd like to see / how you'd like it to be presented, feel free to email me at andyt338@gmail.com.
