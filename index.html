What's HoxTok?

What do we do?

HoxTok or "Hawks Talk" focuses on data visualization for all things Seahawks. If anything, the goal is to persuade you that a trend is affecting us either in a negative or positive way. The point is to spread propaganda in a way that's interactive, persuasive, and gets you thinking.
